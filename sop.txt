Service Level Agreement (SLA):
A Service Level Agreement defines the expected time to resolve customer issues. It ensures that problems are handled within a specific time frame and helps maintain service quality and customer satisfaction.

Root Cause Analysis (RCA):
Root Cause Analysis is used to identify the main cause of a problem. It focuses on understanding why an issue occurred and helps prevent the same problem from happening again in the future.

Audit Process:
Audit is the process of reviewing operations and ensuring that all procedures are followed correctly. It helps maintain quality, compliance, and consistency in processes.
